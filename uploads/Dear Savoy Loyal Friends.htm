Dear Savoy Loyal Friends,
We are delighted to announce that the construction work on the new Savoy Hotel will restart as early as next month, January 2016, after a deferred period of time.  This is fantastic news and something that we very much wanted to share with you all.  

The new Savoy Hotel will bring a renewed energy to the island of Madeira in the very near future and promises to be one of the best hotels in Portugal with the highest levels of hospitality and service to match the long reputable tradition of the former charismatic Savoy Hotel.

This new chapter brings with it a further change in the administration of SIET Savoi SA, which from now on will be the responsibility of the Madeiran Group, AFA.  The Group has an excellent reputation and international experience in various areas of business and management; and shares the same unconditional values of legendary prestige of our Savoy brand.

Therefore, an exciting phase in the story of the Savoy Hotel Group is fast approaching and we will be pleased to provide you with regular progress updates.

As always we are grateful for your attention.
Warmest regards The Savoy Team
